# Islam Stories — Universe Map
**The full character and era structure of the shared universe**
**READ BY:** series planning, crossover design, episode outlines

---

## The Universe at a Glance

7 phases. 14 centuries. One connected civilizational story.
Each phase is a self-contained era with its own character series.
Crossover events are historically authenticated — they are in
Al-Tabari, Ibn Kathir, Baha ad-Din. They are not invented.

## Crossover Mechanics

Every character series is completely standalone.
No prior viewing required. You can start anywhere.

The crossover episodes reward viewers who have watched both series.
They are the same historical event from two different perspectives.
Both episodes work alone. Together they are a different experience.

Crossover events are authenticated in primary sources.
They are not invented to serve the narrative.
The narrative exists because they are in Al-Tabari.
