# Ethical Framework

## What We Charge For
Infrastructure, maintenance, and the classification
work that makes the corpus queryable. Not the scholarship.

## What We Don't Own
The words of Al-Tabari, Ibn Kathir, Imam Bukhari.
These belong to the scholars and to Islamic civilization.

## Accessibility Commitment
- Free tier covers students and individual researchers
- Institutional pricing is nominal (cost recovery only)
- Organizations that cannot afford access: contact us
